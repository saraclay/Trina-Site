Wireframe Tools

A wireframe, also known as a page schematic or screen blueprint, is a visual guide that represents the skeletal framework of a website or screen of a mobile app. It is a low-fidelity visual representation of the layout of our app. The wireframe depicts the page layout or arrangement of the website's or app's content, including interface elements and navigational systems, and how they work together. The wireframe usually lacks typographic style, color, or graphics, since the main focus lies in functionality, behavior, and priority of content. In other words, it focuses on what a screen does, not what it looks like. Wireframes can be pencil drawings or sketches on a whiteboard, or they can be produced by means of a broad array of free or commercial software applications.

Since new ones are always being development, search for wireframe tools in your favorite web search engine.

Sketch

Sketch gives you the power, flexibility and speed you always wanted in a lightweight and easy-to-use package. Finally you can focus on what you do best: Design. Use it to design interfaces, websites, and icons. It's available for the Mac only.

Pencil

Pencil is an open-source GUI prototyping tool that's available for ALL platforms. It is built for the purpose of providing a free and open-source GUI prototyping tool that people can easily install and use to create mockups in popular desktop platforms. They have stencils and templates for Android, iOS Wireframe, and iOS GUI.

Interactive Prototyping

Various tools let you create a clickable prototype by using hotspots to click an area on one screen to another screen. Some tools will create images for you, and some tools will need you to upload images of each screen.

Proto.io

Proto.io Create fully-interactive high-fidelity prototypes that look and work exactly like your app should. No coding required. 15-day free trial. For every friend you invite and signs up for Proto.io you will get 1 extra week of free trial, while your friends will get bonus of an additional two weeks trial when they sign up!

ProtoShare

ProtoShare is an easy-to-use, collaborative prototyping tool that helps teams visualize requirements with website wireframes and interactive prototypes while working together in real-time. Start creating mockups, website wireframes and prototypes today. Free 30-day trial.

FluidUI

Fluid UI will create interactive prototypes and wireframes of Android, iOS and Windows 8 apps. It allows you to share mobile app prototypes with your team and test on phone or tablet. With your free account, you get 1 project with 10 screens, 10MB uploads, unlimited sharing, and mobile testing. It is a browswer-based tool.

Axure

Axure is the standard in interactive wireframe software and gives you the power to quickly and easily deliver much more than typical mockup tools. Generate an interactive HTML website wireframe or UI mockup without coding. Then, send a link to clients or users to review. Or design an Android or iPhone app interface and view it right on your mobile device. Look on Blackboard for the Licensee and Keys.

Flinto

Flinto will make iOS and Android prototypes from your existing screen designs. Design screens in an image tool such as Photoshop, Illustrator, or Pencil. Install prototypes on your mobile device, with your icon. No extra apps required. Your Flinto prototypes look so realistic, people might think they actually are.

InVision

InVision will transform your designs into beautiful, interactive web and mobile mockups. Design screens in an image tool such as Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, or Pencil. You can save your Sketch source files to InVision and automatically turn your artboards into screens. Upload the images into InVision and create hotspots to create an interactive mockup. Share them with your team, clients, and users.
